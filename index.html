ABOUT US

For over 25 years, we've been Pakistan's trusted name in fuel dispensing systems, having installed 1000+ dispensers nationwide. Founded by Mr. M. Shafiq and later joined by his brothers, we combine hands-on experience with innovative technology to deliver reliable fueling solutions.

We specialize in complete fuel management systems, handling everything from crude oil to edible oils. Our new smart technology allows remote monitoring via mobile apps, bringing automation and real-time control to fuel stations.

From mechanical dispensers to IoT-based systems, we offer end-to-end solutions including station design, approvals, and installation. Our turnkey approach ensures seamless operations for both traditional and smart fueling stations.
